## 快捷键（Mac版）
|快捷键|作用|
|---|:---|
|两次Shift|查找类等，很好用|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>F</kbd>|查找|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>R</kbd>|替换|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>[</kbd>|Back|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>]</kbd>|Forward|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+鼠标左键单击|跳转至定义处|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>*</kbd>|列编辑|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>/</kbd>|注释/取消注释|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>N</kbd>|生成代码|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>D</kbd>|复制当前行至下一行|
|<kbd>Command</kbd>+<kbd>Option</kbd>+<kbd>L</kbd>|美化代码|
|<kbd>Option</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>⬆️/⬇️</kbd>|移动当前行至上/下一行|
|<kbd>Option</kbd>+<kbd>Enter</kbd>|导入包/自动修改|
